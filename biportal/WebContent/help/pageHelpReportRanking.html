The Report Ranking and Comments page enables you to rate the selected report in several categories, provide comments and reply to comments from other users. In addition, you also can see the statistics of a report, such as the number of users of the report, the number of users who have scheduled the report, and the number of comments entered for the report.

accessibility

Open the page by clicking on the ☆ "star" icon located to the right of the report with the other action icons. This icon can be found in Cognos search results, My Reports, Control book, and Cognitive Search areas of the portal.

Rating a Report

  1. Move the mouse over the stars of a category. The rating will dynamically display from 0.1 to 5.0 to the right of the the stars. When you get to the appropriate rating number, left click the stars to set the rating. Do the same action on the other categories. When all the categories are rated, the progress bar located under the categories will show 100%. Click the "Rate now" link to submit your rating. You can only submit rankings once per one report.

Add Report Comments

  1. Click the Add Comment button to open the comment dialog box. Type your comments and click Submit. The maximum input length is 500 characters. You are permitted to add one comment per report.
  2. Click the Cancel button to hide the input comment dialog box.

Reply to an existing comment

  1. You can click the Reply link located below an existing comment. Then type your reply in the dialog box and click Send to submit. The maximum input length is 500 characters.
