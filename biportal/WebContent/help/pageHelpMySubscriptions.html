My subscriptions page help

Your may subscribe to a Report Library report to receive e-Mail notification whenever that report is updated. The My subscriptions page enables you to manage any subscriptions you have already set up.

Use the following procedure to work with a subscription:

  1. In the navigation section of the BI@IBM controls portlet, click My subscriptions to open the page.
  2. Locate the subscription you want to work with.

Note: The most common task is to delete a subscription (). Click this icon when you no longer want to be notified when the report is published and it will be removed from the Subscriptions list. You can also execute other Report Library report functions from your subscription list such as view the report, e-Mail it, add it as a favourite in your My Reports portlet or, when available, view information about the report.
